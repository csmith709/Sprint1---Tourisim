George Street Fest

One whole week of spectacular music and the most bars per sqare foot in the world. George Street is home to many events, but George Street Festival hosts both local and internationl musicians, not an event you'll want to miss!


St. John's

Last week of July

Royal St.John's Regatta

Visited by about 50,000 people each year, this one day event has been called the "largest garden party in the world" as it is as much about socialization as it is sport. The regatta shows off Newfoundland's unique rowing style as teams race across Quidi Vidi Lake while vendors line the shore with food, games, goods and music.
