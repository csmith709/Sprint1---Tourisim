Where to Eat
Best of NL Cuisine

Best Coast Restaurant

Located within the Hew & Draw Hotel, Best Coast Restaurant has seen massive success since opening their doors in 2019

Located on West Street in the heart of Corner Brook, you will not be dissappointed by this modern brewpub. Traditional diner style breakfasts, a full lunch menu and a mouth watering dinner offering


Pollen Nation Farm - Cafe and Beestro

Chef Nathan Hornidge offers up a wide variety of cafe items for more casual dining, and a delicious menu of fusion items for the "Beestro" showcasing local NL ingredients mixed with international flavours.

Find them in Little Rapids, within the Humber Valley


Roots, Rants and Roars

The province's famed annual food festival hosted on the third weekend in September in Elliston - Newfoundland. This two-day food festival is a celebration of the provinces food, culture, land, and sea, and is attended by approximately 700 people yearly.

Roots, Rants and Roars has grown to become the premier culinary event in Newfoundland and Labrador and has become recognized throughout Canada as one of the top national culinary festivals


the Boreal Diner

The Boreal Diner is a casual fine-dining restaurant in a beautifully restored heritage building in Bonavista, Newfoundland.

Our small menu changes often, and is focussed on serving local seafood, meat and vegetables. We aim for fresh, clean flavours, enabling you to experience the taste of place unique to Bonavista


Bare Mountain Coffee House

Bare Mountain Coffee House opened in June 2019, at 53 Memorial Drive, Clarenville

Offering an array of tempting artisan baked goodies and gourmet sandwiches, wraps, and traditionally made soup. Sourcing the freshest local ingredients whenever possible to enjoy the best of Newfoundland's taste of place


Portage

Voted #42/100 of Canadas 100 Best Restaurants, husband and wife restauranteur duo Ross Larkin and Celeste Mah opened Portage in 2023. The newest restaurant to be featured, Portage offers an elevated palate without the fuss of a fine dining restaurant.

The culinary style is Newfoundland Canadiana, with global influences from both travel and familial heritage


Mallard Cottage

Situated in a Heritage property in the heart of Quidi Vidi Village, Mallard Cottage offers diners a first class dining experience. From the truly coveted cake table (one plate, one visit - stack it as high as you possibly can) to the daily changing dinner menu.

The award winning chefs use only the best seafood, wild game, and produce available to serve up a fine dining experience, in a rustic down home setting.
